Track your expense with no expense!

Welcome to Expenso! This is a highly user-friendly app to help you manage your personal expenses. With this app, you can calculate your net expenses and view your past transactions.

How To Use: Type in a name for your expense/income as well as the amount in the add transactions tab. Our app will automatically calculate your total expenses, income, and balance. If you made a mistake with a transaction, you can simply delete it in the history tab.
